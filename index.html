My Time at CSULB

I am currently a 4th year student here at CSULB. This is my 4th semester as a CS Major, having come from Computer Engineering previously. My time at this university has been eventful, both socially and academically. I have made friends here that I believe will be lifelong friends, and have made academic progress that I did not think I could accomplish years ago.

I have been a full-time student these past years and since my switch to CS 2 years ago, I have learned Java, C++, and C extensively. I've managed to make it this far with the help and encouragement of my peers. CSULB has been an experience of a lifetime for me.
